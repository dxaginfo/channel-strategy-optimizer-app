Audience Behavior Prediction

Strategic Recommendations
Emerging Opportunities
  • Mobile-first content: Expected to grow 37% in the next 2 years
  • Short-form video: Rapid adoption across all demographics
  • Interactive experiences: Growing engagement in streaming platforms
Potential Risks
  • Traditional TV: Continued decline of 8-12% annually
  • Cable subscriptions: Accelerating cord-cutting trends
  • Platform fragmentation: Increased audience fatigue with multiple subscriptions
Recommended Actions
  • Increase investment in mobile and streaming channels by 25% over next 2 years
  • Develop short-form content strategy for social media platforms
  • Create a cross-platform distribution strategy to mitigate fragmentation risks
  • Establish presence on emerging platforms to reach younger demographics
